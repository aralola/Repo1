Brownies

List Of Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Nutella
  • Eggs

Steps:

  1. Put flour inside a bowl
  2. Crack 3 eggs
  3. Pour 250g of nutella in the bowl
  4. Mix it
  5. Pour the mixture into a baking pan
  6. Bake at 175 degrees celsius for 15 minutes
  7. Enjoy!

Chips

List of ingredients

  • Chips can be prepared from green plantain,yam,potatoes,etc
  • 2 fingers green plantain (yam/potatoes)
  • 50ml.vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Wash and peel the plantain (yam/potatoes).
  2. Slice thinly
  3. Season slightly with salt.
  4. Fry in deep oil until it is crisp and light brown
  5. Drain in a clean basket or kithen paper.
  6. Serve hot. Enjoy!

Akara balls

List of ingredients

  • 1 cup of beans (cowpea)
  • 1 small onion
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Pick the beans and wash to remove beans coats.
  2. Grind into a very smooth paste.
  3. Stir in a big bowl using turning stick,adding a little water at a times.This incoporates air.
  4. Continue stirring until the mixture becomes light and fluffy.
  5. Add chopped onions,pepper and salt to taste.mix it thoroughly.
  6. Fry in deep hot oil until golden brown.
  7. Serve hot.Enjoy!

Pineapple drink

List of ingredients

  • 1/4 of medium sized pineapple
  • 1 table spoon of lime juice
  • Syrup to taste
  • 280ml clean cold water

Steps:

  1. Wash the whole pineapple.rinse in salty water.
  2. Cut off the required size
  3. Peel and grate the pineapple.
  4. Add water to the grated pineapple,pass through a claen seazer.
  5. Add lime juice,syrup and water.mix properly.
  6. Chill and serve.

Soya bean milk

List of ingredients

  • 1 cup of soyabean
  • 2 1/2 cups of clean warm water
  • Sugar to taste
  • Vanilla flavour(or any flavour of your choice).

Steps:

  1. Pick the soya bean properly.
  2. Wash and soak over-night.
  3. Wash the soya bean and remove the bean coats.
  4. Grind the soya bean into smooth paste.
  5. Add water and mix well.
  6. Strain through a clean strainer then through a clean cloth.
  7. Boil the mix for 15-20 minutes.
  8. Add sugar and flavouring to taste
  9. Serve chilled.
